We are asked to solve \(G(t) = 815\) for \(t\)
\begin{align*} G(t) \amp = 815\\ \\ 38e^{.10571 t} \amp = 815\\ \\ e^{.10571 t} \amp = \frac{815}{38} \quad \textrm{(apply the natural log to both sides)}\\ \\ .10571t \amp = \ln\left(\frac{815}{38}\right) \quad (\textrm{recall} \knowl{./knowl/obs-apply-log.html}{\text{Item 5}} \: \textrm{here,} \: x=.10571t)\\ \\ t \amp = \frac{\ln\left(\frac{815}{38}\right)}{.10571}\\ \\ t \amp \approx 29 \end{align*}
MLD will reach 81,500 site visits approximately 29 days after the start of the ad campaign?
