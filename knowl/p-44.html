If a company's cost, revenue, or profit model is linear, then the corresponding marginal funciton will simply be the slope (as we found in the example above). The units for the output values of the marginal functions will therefore be same as those for the slope of the origianl business model. In subsequent chapters we will see that if our business model is NOT linear, the correspondong marginal function will in general NOT be constant.
