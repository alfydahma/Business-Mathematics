Example 2.4.3.

Suppose the price and demand for an item are related by \(p = D(q) = 150 - 6q^2\) where \(p = D(q) \) is the price in dollars and \(q\) is the number of items demanded in hundreds. Suppose also that price and supply are related by \(p= S(q) = 10q^2 + 2q\) where \(p = S(q)\) is again price in dollars and \(q\) is quantity supplied in hundreds.
