Definition 3.2.2.

The average rate of change (AROC) of \(f\) as \(x\) goes from \(x_1\) to \(x_2\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \textrm{AROC} = \dfrac{f(x_2) - f(x_1)}{x_2 - x_1}, \end{equation*}
which is also the slope of the secant line through the points \((x_1,f(x_1))\) and \((x_2,f(x_2))\text{.}\)
