Remark 5.3.2. Summary of Simple Interest Formulas.

  1. Future Value: \(F = P(1+rt)\)
  2. Interest: \(I = F - P = Prt\)
  3. Present Value: \(P = \frac{F}{1+rt}\)
Notice that in this context \(P\) is the principal amount invested or borrowed, and the present value. The future value \(F\) is sometimes referred to as the maturity value.
