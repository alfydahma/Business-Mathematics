Salvage value is the book value of an asset after all depreciation has been fully expensed. The salvage value of an asset is based on what a company expects to receive in exchange for selling or parting out the asset at the end of its useful life. Mingy's truck has a salvage value of $5,500. How long will it take for Mingy's truck to reach its salvage value? Round \(t\) to 2 decimal places then round up to the nearest whole year to answer.
