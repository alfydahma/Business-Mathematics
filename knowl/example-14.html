Example 2.1.2.

Let \(f(x) = x^2 -x +1\text{.}\) For homework find the following. The last two parts will involve \(a\)'s and \(h\)'s. In other words, your answers will be symbolic and not numbers.
  1. \(f(0)\) Solution.
  2. \(f(-2)\) Solution.
  3. \(f(5a)\) Solution.
  4. \(f(a+h)\) Solution.
