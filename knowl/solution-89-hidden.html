\begin{align*} L'(t) \amp = 0 \\ t^2 - 20t + 91 \amp =0 \\ (t-7)(t-13) \amp = 0 \end{align*}

\begin{equation*} L''(t) = 2t -20. \end{equation*}

  • \(\displaystyle L''(13) = 6 > 0\)
