Solution d.1.

\begin{align*} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x^2 - 4x}{2x} \: dx \amp = \int \left( \dfrac{x^2}{2x} - \dfrac{4x}{2x} \right) \: dx = \int \left( \dfrac{1}{2}x - 2 \right) \: dx \\ \\ \amp = \dfrac{1}{2} \int x \: dx - 2 \int \: dx = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2}x^2 - 2x + c \\ \\ \amp = \dfrac{1}{4}x^2 - 2x + c \end{align*}
